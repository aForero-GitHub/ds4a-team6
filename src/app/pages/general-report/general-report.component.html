Nacional Report

In this section you will find 3 graphs that hope to give a summary of the national panorama regarding the patients registered in the entity and who have a diagnosis of hypertension.

- In the upper part you will find the number of patients that the regression algorithm has analyzed for the generation of the new hypertension diagnoses as well as for the creation of the following graphs.

- In the first graph you will find the trend that has taken place in recent months regarding the number of patients with a diagnosis of hypertension by gender.

- Also, in the chart below you can see the top 3 cities with the highest cases of hypertension.

- Finally, you will find a bar chart with the 3 risk factors with the highest presence in the patients analyzed, all these charts are formulated according to the data provided by the entity.
